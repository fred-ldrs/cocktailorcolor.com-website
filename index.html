    About Cocktail or Color?

    What makes this quiz tricky?

    Both cocktails and colours follow the same naming pattern — an evocative adjective paired with a vivid noun. Blue Lagoon is a real cocktail; Cobalt Blue is a real paint colour. Without prior knowledge, the names are almost indistinguishable.

    Are the cocktail names real?

    Yes. Every cocktail in the game is an established recipe — from classics like White Russian and Pink Lady to lesser-known gems like Green Ghost and Brown Derby. No names were invented.

    Are the colour names real?

    All colour names are used in design, fashion, or paint systems — Midnight Blue, Burnt Orange, Sage Green, and so on. They were chosen because they share the same stylistic register as the cocktails.

    How many questions are in the game?

    Each round draws 10 random questions from a pool of 40 names (20 cocktails + 20 colours), so every game is different. Hit Play again to get a fresh mix.
